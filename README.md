# Sales & Revenue Dashboard

## Overview

The Sales & Revenue Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution developed in Power BI to analyze sales performance, revenue trends, product performance, category-wise analysis, and regional sales distribution. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor key business metrics and make data-driven decisions.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and revenue performance.
- Monitor revenue trends over time.
- Compare revenue across regions and product categories.
- Identify top-performing products.
- Track quantity sold across different categories.
- Generate actionable business insights.

---

## Dataset Information

The dashboard is built using the following fields:

Column Name| Description
Order Date| Date of purchase
Product| Product name
Category| Product category
Quantity| Number of units sold
Unit Price| Price per unit
Revenue| Total revenue generated
Region| Sales region

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Quantity Sold
- Average Revenue per Order

---

## Dashboard Components

Revenue by Region

Displays revenue generated across different regions, helping identify high-performing markets.

Revenue by Category

Compares revenue generated by each product category.

Top Products by Revenue

Highlights products contributing the highest revenue.

Monthly Revenue Trend

Shows revenue performance over time and helps identify growth patterns.

Monthly Quantity Trend

Displays sales volume trends across different months.

Quantity Sold by Category

Analyzes product demand across categories.

Revenue Contribution by Category

Shows the percentage contribution of each category to total revenue.

Revenue by Region and Category (Matrix)

Provides a detailed breakdown of revenue across regions and categories for comparative analysis.

The Sales & Revenue Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance through sales, revenue, product, category, and regional analysis. It enables users to monitor key metrics, discover trends, and make informed business decisions using data-driven insights.
